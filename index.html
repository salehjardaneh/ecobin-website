How EcoBin Works

Our innovative system combines cutting-edge technology with sustainable design to transform waste management.

Camera & AI Classification

A high-resolution camera captures waste images, processed by our custom CNN model trained on thousands of waste items for accurate classification.

Servo Motor Sorting

Precision servo motors rotate the internal compartments to direct waste to the correct bin based on the AI's classification.

Solar Powered Operation

Integrated solar panels provide clean energy, with battery backup for continuous operation even in low-light conditions.

User Feedback System

LED indicators and IoT connectivity provide real-time feedback to users and administrators about bin status and waste statistics.

EcoBin User Manual

Learn how to use, maintain, and troubleshoot EcoBin ‚Äî your AI-powered smart waste sorting solution.

1. Getting Started

  • Place EcoBin in a well-lit area for optimal solar charging.
  • Ensure the internal compartments are clean and empty.
  • Turn on the main power switch located under the lid.

2. How to Use EcoBin

  • Tap the ‚ÄúScan‚Äù button on the front panel to activate the system.
  • Place the waste item under the camera and wait for scanning.
  • EcoBin will classify the item (paper, plastic, or can) and sort it automatically.
  • LED glows green for success, red for an error.

3. Power & Solar System

  • EcoBin charges through a built-in photovoltaic panel.
  • A lithium-ion battery ensures continuous operation in low-light.
  • The system enters low-power sleep mode when inactive.

4. Maintenance & Cleaning

  • Wipe the external surfaces weekly with a damp cloth.
  • Inspect the camera and LED indicators for dust or debris.
  • Modular parts can be replaced using the rear access panel.

5. IoT & Connectivity

  • EcoBin uses IoT to send full-bin alerts to the admin system.
  • Use the n8n automation platform to track bin status remotely.
  • All data is securely transmitted without identifying user info.

6. Safety & Support

  • Rounded edges and weatherproof housing ensure public safety.
  • No exposed wiring; child-safe design.
  • Need help? Use EcoBot (bottom right) or email us directly.
